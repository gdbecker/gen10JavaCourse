    About Hiking Ventures

    Who We Are

    Hiking Ventures is a robust, data-drive web application designed to make your life as simple as possible when it comes to planning your hiking trips. Take the stress and hassle out of planning by using our sophisticated system and data. We get it - you're out there wanting to make memories, have fun, and not worry about the little details. Using Hiking Ventures will streamline your planning so that you can escape the everyday and go on the trip of your dreams.

    How To Use This Site

    Take a look at the snapshot descriptions below to learn what Hiking Ventures is capable of to aid in your trip planning.

    Go the Hiking Trips page to manage all of your trips. Create, view, edit, and delete your trips. You will be able to look at lists of travelers and equipment for each trip plus take a look at the map of the trip route.

    The Trails page is the place to manage trails within the system, so add, view, edit, and delete trail information. Also take a look at location information for each trail and trips associated with each one.

    Here on the Travelers page you will be able to add, view, edit, and delete information for each traveler in the system. Take a look at the all the trips the traveler has been on as well as future trips.

    Go to the Equipment page to add, view, edit, and delete equipment information. Check out photos for each equipment available in the storeroom and trips associated with each equipment type.

    The Locations page is the place to check out more detailed information regarding location specifics. Add, view, edit, and delete its information here. You will be able to see what trails and trips are happening in each location as well.
